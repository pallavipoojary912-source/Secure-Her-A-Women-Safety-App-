Help & Instructions

How do I set up my emergency contacts?

Go to the Emergency Contact page, fill in the details of your trusted contacts, and click "Add Contact". You can add multiple contacts who will be notified in case of emergency.

How does the panic button work?

When you press the panic button, the app will immediately send your current location to all your emergency contacts via SMS and email with a message indicating you need help.

Is my location data secure?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your location data is only shared with your emergency contacts when you explicitly use the panic button or share location feature.

Can I use this app without internet?

The panic button will attempt to send alerts via SMS if internet is not available, but for full functionality including GPS tracking, internet connection is required.
